Port town and capital of Ambervale. A group of adventurers bought out
the previous ruler a hundred years ago, and [Ares
Dragmaar](The_King "wikilink") has been crowned king since then. Famed
for it\'s [fortified wine](Kingsblood "wikilink") and in decades past as
a centre for adventurers to gather. In the past \~70 years the land has
been very peaceful, with no recorded wars or invasions.
